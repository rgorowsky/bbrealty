About BB Realty

I’m a Minneapolis native born and raised. Working as BOTH an interior designer and a licensed Realtor.

I grew up watching This Old House and Martha Stewart Living, and they helped shape the designer I am today. I LOVE the character & craftsmanship of older homes. My goal in design is to make these old homes functional spaces tailored to each client’s lifestyle. As a realtor, My goal is always the same: to help you feel cozy, excited, and completely at home—wherever your next chapter takes you. Let’s chat about how I can help bring your vision to life.

-Betsy Bissonette, Founder BB Realty
